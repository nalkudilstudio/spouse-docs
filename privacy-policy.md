## Overview

Spouse ("the App") is built on a foundation of trust and privacy. This policy explains clearly what data we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it. We designed this app for two people in a relationship — your data is yours, and we treat it accordingly.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Account information**: Name, email address, and profile photo provided via Google Sign-In.
- **Relationship content**: Journal entries, compliments, AMA answers, bucket list items, goals, mood check-ins, pulse status updates, and any other content you create within the app.
- **Partner pairing**: A unique couple ID generated when two accounts are linked.
- **Profile preferences**: Nicknames, anniversary date, birthday, and app settings.

### Information Collected Automatically
- **Usage data**: Basic activity logs used to calculate the Love Meter score and badge milestones.
- **Device information**: Android version and device model, collected by Firebase for crash reporting.

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to operate the Spouse app. Specifically:

- To sync your content between you and your partner's device in real time.
- To power features like the Love Meter, Badges, Weekly Recap, and Monthly Time Vault.
- To send push notifications (nudges, reminders) that you have opted into.
- To improve app stability through anonymised crash reports.

**We do not sell your data. We do not share your data with advertisers. We do not use your content for AI training.**

---

## 3. Data Encryption

All sensitive content — journal entries, compliments, AMA answers, bucket list items, and mood entries — is **encrypted on-device** before being stored in Firebase. Your couple ID is used as the encryption context, meaning your data is only readable within your private couple partition.

---

## 4. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on **Google Firebase (Firestore)**, hosted in Google's cloud infrastructure. Firebase complies with GDPR, ISO 27001, and SOC 1/2/3 certifications.

---

## 5. Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services:

| Service | Purpose |
|---|---|
| Google Firebase (Auth) | Secure sign-in |
| Google Firebase (Firestore) | Data storage and real-time sync |
| Google Firebase (Cloud Messaging) | Push notifications |

No other third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs are included.

---

## 6. Data Retention

Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. If you disconnect your partner pairing or delete your account, you may contact us at **nalkudilstudio@gmail.com** to request permanent deletion of your data.

---

## 7. Children's Privacy

Spouse is intended for users aged 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 18. If we become aware that a user under 18 has created an account, we will delete their data promptly.

---

- Request correction of inaccurate data.
- Request deletion of your data.
- Object to certain processing of your data.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at **nalkudilstudio@gmail.com**.

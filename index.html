Getting Started

Up & Running in 3 Steps

From download to your first nudge — here's exactly how to get started together.

01
📲

Download & Sign In

Get Spouse from the Google Play Store and sign in securely with your Google account.

  • Search "Spouse — Nalkudil Studio" on Play Store
  • Tap Install → Open
  • Tap "Sign in with Google"
  • Select your Google account
  • Allow notification permissions when prompted
02
👤

Set Up Your Profile

Personalise your space before inviting your partner — it makes the experience feel like yours.

  • Set a nickname for yourself
  • Add your partner's name
  • Enter your anniversary date
  • Add both birthdays
  • Enable biometric lock (optional but recommended)
03
🔗

Pair With Your Partner

Spouse creates a private, encrypted space just for the two of you using a unique pairing code.

  • One partner opens Menu → Pairing
  • Tap "Generate Code" — share it with your partner
  • Partner enters the code on their device
  • Both devices confirm the pairing
  • Your private space is now active 💚

Feature Guide

Know Your Tools

Seven features worth understanding deeply — they form the heart of the Spouse experience.

💚
Nudge
Connection

The fastest way to say "I'm thinking of you" without interrupting their day. One tap sends a warm notification to your partner's phone.

  • 4 quick nudge options on the Home screen
  • Each nudge bumps your Love Meter score
  • Your partner sees a teal glow notification
  • No reply needed — it's just a heartbeat
✨
AMA — Ask Me Anything
Discovery

A fresh curated question every day. Both partners answer privately — neither can see the other's answer until both have submitted. The reveal is the magic.

  • New question every day at midnight
  • Answer privately — your partner can't peek
  • Both answers reveal simultaneously once both submit
  • Answers earn Love Meter points
  • Previous sparks are archived for you to revisit
❤️
Love Meter
Health Score

A living score (0–100) that reflects how actively you're investing in your relationship. It grows when you engage and gently decays on quiet days.

  • Starts at 50 — grows with every interaction
  • Nudges, AMAs, journals, goals all bump the score
  • Decays slightly on inactive days (can be toggled off)
  • 5 levels — Budding Romance → Legendary Couple
  • Visible on Home screen and Weekly Recap
💚
The Pulse
Real-Time

Let your partner know what you're up to in real time — without texting. A gentle presence indicator that removes the guesswork of "can I call now?"

  • 8 status options — At Work, Heading Home, Free, etc.
  • Updates instantly on your partner's Home screen
  • Pulsing teal dot shows when status is active
  • Tap the mini-card on Home to open full Pulse screen
  • No notification sent — it's ambient, not intrusive
🗓️
Time Vault
Monthly Capsule

A digital time capsule that collects your shared memories throughout the month and unlocks on the 1st — like a monthly gift to yourselves.

  • Add notes, moments and memories all month long
  • Vault seals at end of month — no peeking!
  • Unlocks automatically on the 1st of each month
  • Review together for a beautiful monthly ritual
  • Each month's vault is archived permanently
🔄
Resonance
Compatibility

Your relationship compatibility snapshot — a radar chart across 5 dimensions. It's not a test, it's a mirror to help you understand each other better.

  • 5 dimensions — Values, Habits, Love Language, Fun, Communication
  • Both partners answer independently
  • Results shown as a beautiful radar chart
  • No right or wrong scores — it's about awareness
  • Updates as you both engage more with the app
🤝
Honesty Hub
Safe Space

A structured, calm space for working through disagreements. Not a fight room — a resolution room. Designed to replace heated texts with thoughtful perspective sharing.

  • Raise a topic — both partners share their perspective privately
  • Neither can see the other's view until both have submitted
  • Status tracks — Open, Resolved, Revisit
  • Accessible from Settings → Safe Space
  • Everything is encrypted — this space is just for you two

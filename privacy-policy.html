Section 01

Information We Collect

Information you provide directly:

  • Account details — name, email address, and profile photo via Google Sign-In
  • Relationship content — journal entries, compliments, AMA answers, bucket list items, goals, mood check-ins, and pulse status updates
  • Partner pairing — a unique couple ID generated when two accounts are linked
  • Profile preferences — nicknames, anniversary date, birthday, and app settings

Information collected automatically:

  • Usage activity — basic interaction logs used to calculate your Love Meter score and badge milestones
  • Device information — Android version and device model, collected by Firebase for crash reporting only
Section 02

How We Use Your Data

We use your information solely to operate the Spouse app. Specifically:

  • To sync your content between you and your partner's device in real time
  • To power features like the Love Meter, Badges, Weekly Recap, and Monthly Time Vault
  • To send push notifications (nudges, reminders) that you have opted into
  • To improve app stability through anonymised crash reports

We never: sell your data · share it with advertisers · use your content for AI training · read your private messages or journal entries

Section 03

Data Encryption

All sensitive content is encrypted on-device before being stored in Firebase. This includes:

  • Journal entries (Memories)
  • Compliments (Soul Notes)
  • AMA answers
  • Bucket list items
  • Mood check-in entries

Your couple ID is used as the encryption context, meaning your data is only readable within your private couple partition — not by us, and not by any other user.

Section 04

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on Google Firebase (Firestore), hosted in Google's cloud infrastructure. Firebase complies with GDPR, ISO 27001, and SOC 1/2/3 certifications — among the highest standards in the industry.

Section 05

Third-Party Services

The app uses only the following third-party services — no advertising SDKs, no analytics trackers:

Service Purpose
Google Firebase AuthSecure sign-in via Google
Google Firebase FirestoreEncrypted data storage & real-time sync
Google Firebase Cloud MessagingPush notifications (nudges, reminders)
Section 06

Data Retention

Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. If you disconnect your partner pairing or wish to delete your account entirely, contact us at nalkudilstudio@gmail.com and we will permanently delete your data upon request.

Section 07

Children's Privacy

Spouse is intended for users aged 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 18. If we become aware that a user under 18 has created an account, we will delete their data promptly. If you believe a minor has used the app, please contact us immediately.

Section 08

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request permanent deletion of your data
  • Object to certain processing of your data
  • Data portability — receive your data in a readable format

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at nalkudilstudio@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

Section 09

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time as the app evolves. We will notify you of any significant changes via an in-app notice. The "Last Updated" date at the top of this page will always reflect the most recent version. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
